About

Ian Rice is a Tattoo Artist from Denver, CO with 13+ years of experience in traditional tattooing disciplines with a focus on modern illustration.

His passion for the craft of tattooing brings the experience of modern standards with traditional sensibility. He believes that getting a tattoo should be a positive experience and loves working with his clients to create a unique experience.

Ian is currently interested in medium to large scale illustrative / traditional pieces. Medium to large script tattoos are also priority.

Free quotes for artwork and tattoos are aways available!
